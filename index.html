Lady Gaga-Chromatica

Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta also known as "Lady Gaga" is an American singer and songwriter who has won 11 Grammy awards.


Her latest album, Chromatica, was released on May 19th . The album's 4th track, "Rain on Me" topped the US Billboard Hot 100 at #1
